Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our services

How quickly can you help me find a property?

Depending on your requirements, we can typically show you suitable properties within 24-48 hours. For urgent needs, we can arrange viewings the same day.

Do you charge any fees to buyers?

No, our services are completely free for buyers. We earn our commission from the property sellers, so you don't pay anything for our assistance.

Can you help with property documentation?

Yes, we provide complete assistance with all legal documentation, RERA compliance, and ensure all paperwork is in order before finalizing any transaction.

What areas do you cover in East Pune?

We cover all major areas in East Pune including Viman Nagar, Kharadi, Koregaon Park, Wadgaon Sheri, Kalyani Nagar, and surrounding localities.

Do you help with home loans?

Yes, we have partnerships with leading banks and financial institutions to help you get the best home loan rates and terms for your property purchase.

Can I schedule property viewings on weekends?

Absolutely! We understand that weekdays can be busy, so we're available for property viewings on weekends and even outside regular business hours.
