Our Story

Founded in 1994 by Laila Jena and Sanjay Jena, Shelters Realty began as a small family business with a simple mission: to help people find not just houses, but homes. What started as a passion for real estate in East Pune has grown into one of the region's most respected property consultancies.

Over the decades, we've witnessed East Pune transform from a quiet suburb into a thriving hub of IT parks, educational institutions, and residential communities. Through every change, we've remained committed to our core values of integrity, transparency, and personalized service.

Today, as the city continues to evolve, we're proud to be at the forefront of East Pune's real estate landscape, helping both first-time buyers and seasoned investors navigate the market with confidence and ease.
